Support

Primary:        Assault Rifle
Secondary:      Gremlin

Squaddie Level skills:
    Adrenaline (grant sheild to all allies on kill)
    Intrusion Protocol (Hacking)
    MediEvac (post mission healing for all soldiers, i.e. upto min 50% HP)

Surgeon:
    Recovery Patch I, II, III (Heal targeted ally and grant damage reduction for increasing amounts each turn)
    Stim I, II, III (Revive and heal ally/apply combat stim buff/grant extra action point)

Combat Medic:
    Enhanced adrenaline I, II (Increased shield HP from adrenaline and duration)
    Restoration Protocol (base game)
    Group therapy (Shield/Heal all allies) ala restoration/shield protocol
    Reactive therapy (Ally killed resets cooldown/grants charge for group therapy)

Paragon:
    Empower I, II, II (grant action point and damage boost to 1 ally)
    Teamwork I, II (targetted ally gets APs when killing a target)
    Lock and Load (Each ally reloads and gets damage boost for 1 turn)

Strategist:
    HighPowerShot I, II, III (Special shot with increased damage and progressively decreasing cooldown)
    Weak Point (Kill boosts allies crit chance for 2 turns) ala old adrenaline/combat rush
    Holotargeting (Base game skill)
    Surge (Reset all cooldown for targeted ally including self) ala manual override)

Vanguard

Primary:          Bullpup
Secondary:        Sword

Squaddie Level skills:
    Fortress (Heal 1 HP per turn if injured and increased post mission healing, i.e. upto 3 HP)
    Slash (Basic Sword Attack)

Warden Tree:
    Distraction I, II, III (Mimic beacon like effect, with increased damage reduction and then reduce cooldown)
    Leech (Damage dealt heals by a percentage amount)
    Self Revive (Guaranteed bleedout once per turn and revive at the end of turn on bleeding out)
    Badass (75% damage reduction against 1st attack this enemy turn)

Paladin Tree:
    Bastion (Each ally within 5 tile range of this Heal 1 HP per turn if injured. Passive)
    Inspiration I, II (Grant target unit increased defense until start of next turn)
    Rally I, II, III (Group Vampiric aura with increased amount and duration)
    Stand Together (Area wide revival and damage reduction by 50% until end of turn)

Shock Trooper:
    Fleche (LW2)?
    Intimidate I, II, III (Targeted enemy gets panic roll and knock back)
    Breach (Enemies get breached status: They take increased damage and killing them gives 1 AP )
    Demoralize/unnerve (Damaged enemies get decreased damage)

Assault:
    Executioner (Increased damage against injured enemies)
    Rage Shot I, II, III (Deal Increased damage scaling with missing health)
    Close and Personal (LW2)
    HEMORRHAGE (Crit on enemies applies bleed)
    Avenger (When an ally takes damage, this unit gets +25% Damage for this turn, to a maximum of 4 stacks. When an ally dies, this unit gets 3 Actions.)

Heavy:

Primary:          Cannon
Secondary:        Grenade Launcher


Squaddie skills:
    Traverse Fire (LW2)
    Anchor (stacking aim and damage buff when taking a shot, max 3, removed when moving)

Specialist:
    Redeploy I, II (Activate to get 3 move actions only without losing anchored buffs/decrease cooldown)
    Fury (Missing or grazing triggers another shot)
    Refreshing Reload (Reload reduced all cooldowns by 1. Once per turn)
    Streak (On kill, gain 1 stack of the Streak status effect. When reaching 5 stacks of Streak, this unit gains 1 Action and loses all stacks)
    Preparation (35% chance to gain an achored stack each turn)
    Payback (Gain crit chance when taking damage during enemy turn for 1 turn)

Artillery:
    Reckless shot I, II, III (Increased crit chance (and aim?) shot)
    Heat Up I, II (Activate to increase damage by 25%/Auto reload)
    Banish
    Embers (20% chance to gain heat up at start of turn when it is on cooldown)

Defender:
    Ever Vigilant
    Cool Under Pressure
    At the Ready (Overwatch reduces cooldown of all abilities by 1)
    Sentinel
    Counter Push (Kills on overwatch/suppression grant +3 mob (non-stacking) on the next turn)
    Slayer (Increased damage on OW and suppression shots)

Demolitionist (most likely one to get grenade perks from base game mixed in):
    Explosive shot I, II (special shot that explodes target enemy)
    Dig in I, II (Activated ability to grant surrounding allies +10/+20 aim)
    Demolition (base game)
    Heavy Frags I, II, III (Grenades get bonus use, have increased damage and increased radius)

Secondary:        Knife


Squaddie skills:
    Cloak (Coneal with a cooldown/charges? Infinite duration or ?)

Raider:

Recon:

    Obfuscate (Conceal target non-phantom ally)

Commando (map to secondary weapon):

    Silent Stab (1st Knife kill per turn does not break concealment?)
    Cutthroat
    Ambush (Passive mob and damage boost when concealed)
    Regenerative cloak (Heal when cloaking)
    Relentless (First knife kill per turn gives 1 AP back)

Sniper

Primary:          Sniper
Secondary:        pistol

Squaddie skills:
    Squadsight

Marksman:
    First blood I, II, III (Special shot that does 1.5/1.75/2x damage boost vs enemies at full health)
    Spree (25% chance to get 2 actions back after a kill/crit kill, once per turn)
    Alpha (Increased damage on the first shot of magazine)
    Extra round (crits grant +1 ammo)

Assassin:

    Omega (Increased damae on last shot of magazine)
    Head Hunter (kills grant +3% damage to a 30% cap)

Stalker:

    Terrified (Passive chance to panic/disorient enemies on kill/crit aka Apex Predator)
    Sure Thing (Passive: Any shot that's not a crit increases crit chance, until you crit)
    Active Reload (When this unit uses Reload on an empty magazine, this unit deals +25% Damage for 2 turns or until reloading)?

Hunter:
    Fast Fingers I, II (Passive: Kill reload weapon and then grants action)
    Chain Shot I, II (2nd shot if the first hits and then reduced cooldown)
